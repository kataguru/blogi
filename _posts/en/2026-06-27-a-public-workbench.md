This blog starts as a practice version. The goal is not to build a perfect site, but a functional public desktop.

A good blog is not born from the layout, but from repetition: observations, models and unfinished thoughts are published so that they leave a usable trace.

## What's up here?

Texts with some structure are especially suitable here:

- observation
- a problem
- model
- practical conclusion
- repair shop

Topics can include artificial intelligence, local models, systems thinking, testing, health, resilience, writing and hiking.

## What should not be brought here

It is not a good idea to transfer a private diary, sensitive information or material that you would later regret publishing here.

A public site works best when its unit is a clear thought, not a crude internal outburst.

## The first principle

The smallest sufficient release wins over a complete but unreleased system.
