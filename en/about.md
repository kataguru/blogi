# About me

I am Jari Katajisto: software tester, entrepreneur, AI enthusiast, writer and hiker.

My interests are focused on local artificial intelligence models, systems thinking, resilience, health, writing and building functional structures for everyday life.

This blog is a public desktop. I write about things that I try to understand or formulate into a usable form.

## Principles

- less noise
- more structure
- practical models before opinions
- public thinking, but not at the expense of the private
